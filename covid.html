Covid-19

Due to the ongoing pandemic that is caused by Covid-19, we at the museum must always follow the rules from the government. On this page you can keep yourself up to date on what rules apply at the museum at any given time.

As for now we urge every visitor to follow the 1 meter rule and wash your hands regurarly. At each exhibition and event we have automatic hand sanitizer stands at the entry and exit.

If you have any questions regarding the rules and what rules applies, you are more than welcome to contact us.
